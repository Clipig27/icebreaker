## Description

Whether you're hanging out on a call, chilling with roommates, or at a party — Icebreaker gives your group something to do besides scroll. 10 games designed for friend groups of any size, from chill to chaotic.

No sign-ups. No ads. Just pick a username, make a room, and start playing in seconds.

GAMES INCLUDED:

- TRUST ME BRO — Each player shares a set of statements. One might be a lie. Can you spot it? Read the room, trust your gut, and call out the bluffs.

- SILLY SPOTLIGHT — Step into the spotlight. Players perform silly challenges and the audience votes across 3 rounds. Fame or flame-out — you decide.

- COPYCAT — Everyone answers a prompt at the same time. Give a unique answer and score big. Give the same answer as someone else? You both lose points. Think different or think again.

- NUMBER GUESSOR — How many bones are in the human body? How far is the moon? Guess the number closest to the real answer. No trivia knowledge required — just vibes and estimation.

- PIE CHARTS — Vote on "who's most likely to..." questions about your group, then watch the results unfold as pie charts. It's part game, part group therapy.

- DEAL OR STEAL — Negotiate, bluff, and betray. Players make deals in secret, but will they honor them or steal everything? Trust is a currency, and it runs out fast.

- SHADOW PROTOCOL — A social deduction game where agents carry out missions while shadows try to sabotage from within. Discuss, accuse, and deduce before it's too late.

- SMARTY POT — Trivia with a twist. A shared prize pot grows each round, but only correct answers keep you in. Push your luck or play it safe — the pot doesn't wait.

- LINK OR SINK — Build a chain of linked words, one player at a time. Think a link is bogus? Challenge it and an AI referee makes the call. Keep the chain alive or watch it snap.

- PLOT TWIST — Collaborative storytelling where everyone adds to a growing story. Bait others into using your secret words to score points. The best twists win.

HOW IT WORKS:

1. Open Icebreaker and pick a username.
2. Create a room or join one with a room code.
3. The host picks a game and everyone plays on their own phone.
4. Laugh, argue, and crown the winner.

No accounts, no emails, no friction. Works on any iPhone — everyone plays on their own screen.

Icebreaker is built for hangouts, road trips, game nights, FaceTime calls, and any time your group needs something fun to do together. Find out who's really the funniest, smartest, and most devious in your friend group.
