## Description

Icebreaker is the ultimate party game app for friend groups. Create a room, share the code, and jump into 10 wildly different games -- all played in real time on your phones.

No sign-ups. No ads. Just pick a username, make a room, and start playing in seconds.

GAMES INCLUDED:

- LIE DETECTOR -- Each player shares a set of statements. One is a lie. Can you spot it? Read the room, trust your gut, and call out the bluffs.

- TALENT SHOW -- Step into the spotlight. Players perform silly challenges and the audience votes across 3 rounds. Fame or flame-out -- you decide.

- STAND OUT -- Everyone answers a prompt at the same time. Give a unique answer and score big. Give the same answer as someone else? Zero points. Think different or think again.

- NUMBER GUESSOR -- How many bones are in the human body? How far is the moon? Guess the number closest to the real answer and claim the points. No trivia knowledge required -- just vibes and estimation.

- PIE CHARTS -- Vote on "who's most likely to..." questions about your group, then watch the results unfold as pie charts. It's part game, part group therapy.

- DEAL OR STEAL -- Negotiate, bluff, and betray. Players make deals in secret, but will they honor them or steal everything? Trust is a currency, and it runs out fast.

- SHADOW PROTOCOL -- A social deduction game where agents carry out missions while shadows try to sabotage from within. Discuss, accuse, and deduce before it's too late.

- POT LUCK -- Trivia with a twist. A shared prize pot grows each round, but only correct answers keep you in. Push your luck or play it safe -- the pot doesn't wait.

- CHAINLINK -- Build a chain of linked words, one player at a time. Think a link is bogus? Challenge it and an AI referee makes the call. Keep the chain alive or watch it snap.

- PLOT TWIST -- Collaborative storytelling where everyone contributes sentences to a wild story. Steal words from other players' submissions to craft your own narrative. The best twists win.

HOW IT WORKS:

1. Open Icebreaker and pick a username.
2. Create a room or join one with a room code.
3. The host picks a game and everyone plays on their own phone.
4. Laugh, argue, and crown the winner.

No accounts, no emails, no friction. Works on any iPhone -- everyone plays on their own screen.

Icebreaker is built for house parties, road trips, game nights, and any time you need to break the ice. Grab your friends, open the app, and find out who's really the funniest, smartest, and most devious in your group.
