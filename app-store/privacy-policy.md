# Icebreaker Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 2, 2026

Icebreaker ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") is a multiplayer party game application for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App.

By using Icebreaker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Username.** When you use the App, you choose a display name (username). This is the only piece of personally identifiable information you provide. No email address, phone number, or password is required to play.

### 1.2 Information Generated Automatically

- **Anonymous User ID.** We generate a unique, anonymous identifier for your device to manage sessions and gameplay. This ID is not linked to your real-world identity.
- **Game Data.** During gameplay, we collect data related to your in-game actions, such as answers submitted, votes cast, scores, and game state. This data is used solely to facilitate gameplay.
- **Online Presence Status.** We track whether you are currently active in a game room so that other players in your room can see your connection status.

### 1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, physical address, or payment information.
- We do not collect precise or coarse location data.
- We do not collect contacts, photos, or any data from other apps on your device.
- We do not use device advertising identifiers (IDFA).

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Gameplay.** To create and manage game rooms, facilitate real-time multiplayer sessions, display your username to other players in your room, track scores, and deliver game results.
- **Session Management.** To maintain your connection to a game room and restore your session if you temporarily disconnect.
- **App Functionality.** To ensure the App operates correctly and to debug technical issues.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to providing the core gameplay experience.

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

- **With Other Players.** Your chosen username, online presence status, and in-game actions (such as answers and votes) are visible to other players in the same game room. This is essential for multiplayer gameplay.
- **With Service Providers.** We use the following third-party services to operate the App:
  - **Supabase** (database hosting) -- stores usernames, anonymous user IDs, and game room data. Supabase's privacy policy is available at https://supabase.com/privacy.
  - **Railway** (game server hosting) -- hosts our WebSocket-based game server that manages real-time multiplayer sessions. Railway's privacy policy is available at https://railway.app/legal/privacy.
- **We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.**
- **We do not share your data with advertisers.**
- **Legal Requirements.** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal process, such as a subpoena or court order.

---

## 4. Data Retention

- **Game Data.** Game data is ephemeral. Game rooms and all associated data (answers, votes, scores, game state) are automatically deleted when the room is closed. We do not retain historical game data after a session ends.
- **Usernames and User IDs.** Your username and anonymous user ID are stored until you delete your account or request deletion. No game history is retained beyond active sessions.
- **Server Logs.** Minimal server logs may be retained for up to 30 days for debugging and operational purposes. These logs do not contain personally identifiable information.

---

## 5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

- Encrypted connections (TLS/SSL) between the App and our servers.
- Secure WebSocket (WSS) connections for real-time gameplay.
- Access controls on our database and server infrastructure.
- Use of reputable, industry-standard hosting providers (Supabase and Railway).

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 6. Third-Party Analytics and Advertising

- **Analytics.** We do not currently use any third-party analytics services. If we add analytics in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
- **Advertising.** The App does not display advertisements. We do not use ad networks or share data with advertisers.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Icebreaker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided information to us, please contact us at the email address below.

---

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

- **Account Deletion.** You may request deletion of your username and anonymous user ID at any time by contacting us at the email address below. Since game data is ephemeral and deleted when rooms close, there is no persistent game data to delete.
- **Data Access.** You may request a copy of the data we hold about you by contacting us.
- **Opt-Out.** Since we do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking, there is nothing to opt out of.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you may have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, port, or erase your data, and the right to restrict or object to processing. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

If you are a California resident, you may have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

---

## 9. International Data Transfers

Our servers are hosted in the United States. If you access the App from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the App, you consent to such transfer and processing.
