Icebreaker Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2, 2026

Icebreaker ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") is a multiplayer party game application for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App.

By using Icebreaker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Username. When you use the App, you choose a display name (username). This is the only piece of personally identifiable information you provide. No email address, phone number, or password is required to play.

1.2 Information Generated Automatically

  • Anonymous User ID. We generate a unique, anonymous identifier for your device to manage sessions and gameplay. This ID is not linked to your real-world identity.
  • Game Data. During gameplay, we collect data related to your in-game actions, such as answers submitted, votes cast, scores, and game state. This data is used solely to facilitate gameplay.
  • Online Presence Status. We track whether you are currently active in a game room so that other players in your room can see your connection status.

1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, physical address, or payment information.
  • We do not collect precise or coarse location data.
  • We do not collect contacts, photos, or any data from other apps on your device.
  • We do not use device advertising identifiers (IDFA).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Gameplay. To create and manage game rooms, facilitate real-time multiplayer sessions, display your username to other players in your room, track scores, and deliver game results.
  • Session Management. To maintain your connection to a game room and restore your session if you temporarily disconnect.
  • App Functionality. To ensure the App operates correctly and to debug technical issues.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to providing the core gameplay experience.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • With Other Players. Your chosen username, online presence status, and in-game actions (such as answers and votes) are visible to other players in the same game room. This is essential for multiplayer gameplay.
  • With Service Providers. We use Supabase (database hosting) and Railway (game server hosting) to operate the App.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

We do not share your data with advertisers.

Legal Requirements. We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal process.

4. Data Retention

  • Game Data. Game data is ephemeral. Game rooms and all associated data are automatically deleted when the room is closed.
  • Usernames and User IDs. Stored until you delete your account or request deletion.
  • Server Logs. Minimal server logs may be retained for up to 30 days for debugging purposes. These logs do not contain personally identifiable information.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted connections (TLS/SSL) between the App and our servers.
  • Secure WebSocket (WSS) connections for real-time gameplay.
  • Access controls on our database and server infrastructure.
  • Use of reputable, industry-standard hosting providers.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Analytics and Advertising

  • Analytics. We do not currently use any third-party analytics services.
  • Advertising. The App does not display advertisements.

7. Children's Privacy

Icebreaker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided information to us, please contact us at the email address below.

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Account Deletion. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
  • Data Access. You may request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Opt-Out. Since we do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking, there is nothing to opt out of.

If you are located in the EEA, you may have additional rights under the GDPR. If you are a California resident, you may have rights under the CCPA. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, please contact us.

9. International Data Transfers

Our servers are hosted in the United States. If you access the App from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
